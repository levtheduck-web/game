How to Play
🖱️Click a building in the toolbar at the bottom, then click any empty tile on the grid. New buildings must connect to your existing colony.
⚡Everything needs power. Solar Arrays only work in daylight — build Batteries to last the night, or research Fusion Power for 24/7 energy.
🫧💧🍎Watch the O₂, Water, and Food bars at the top. If any hit zero, colonists start dying. Build Oxygen Generators, Water Extractors, and Hydroponics Bays.
🔬Build Research Labs (staffed by colonists) to earn research points. Click the 🔬 button top-right to spend them on better tech and new buildings.
⚠️Random events happen — dust storms, malfunctions, even meteorites! Click a broken (smoking) building to repair it for 40₡.
🎯Click 🎯 to see your objectives. Grow to 100 colonists to win — then keep playing to fully terraform Mars and turn the sky blue!
