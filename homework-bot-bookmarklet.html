📚 Homework Bot — Bookmarklet

No install. Works on school-managed Chrome. Drag the link to your bookmarks bar, click it on any homework page.

1. Configure

Get one at console.anthropic.com (needs credits) Default: claude-sonnet-4-6. Use claude-opus-4-7 for harder problems.
⚠ Your API key is embedded in the bookmark. Anyone who sees your bookmarks can copy it. Don't share screenshots of your bookmarks bar.

2. Drag to your bookmarks bar

Fill in your API key above, then click "Generate".
If you don't see your bookmarks bar in Chrome, press ⌘⇧B (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+B (Win) to show it.

3. Use it

  1. Open your homework in Google Classroom, Khan Academy, IXL, Canvas, myhrw, etc.
  2. Click in the answer field (so it's focused).
  3. Click the 📚 Homework Bot bookmark.
  4. It reads the question, asks Claude, and auto-types the answer for you.
